## Dashboard

Your health metrics at a glance.

### Features

#### Step Tracking

**On Mobile Devices:**
1. Click **"Start Tracking"** button
2. Grant motion sensor permission if prompted
3. Keep the app open or running in background
4. Steps will count automatically as you walk

**Controls:**
- **Start Tracking** - Begin counting steps
- **Stop Tracking** - Pause step counting
- **Reset** - Reset step count to zero

**Note:** Step tracking requires a device with an accelerometer (smartphones, smartwatches).

#### Health Metrics

View your current stats:
- **Steps** - Daily step count
- **Calories** - Estimated calories burned (calculated from steps)
- **Water** - Water intake in liters
- **Sleep** - Sleep duration

#### Daily Check-in

Click **"Daily Check-in"** to mark your daily health check complete. This helps build a consistent health tracking habit.

## Diet Plan

Plan and track your meals throughout the week.

### View Modes

#### Daily View
- See today's meals
- Quick overview of breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
- Click any meal card to mark it complete

#### Weekly View
- Full week at a glance
- Expand/collapse each day
- See all meals for the week
- Track completion across multiple days

#### Monthly View
- Calendar view of the entire month
- See daily completion percentages
- Quick overview of your diet consistency

### Tracking Meals

1. **Click on a meal card** to toggle completion
2. **Green checkmark** indicates completed meal
3. **Points awarded:**
   - ‚úÖ Complete meal: +10 points
   - ‚ùå Skip meal: -5 points

### Progress Tracking

View your stats:
- **Total Points** - Accumulated diet points
- **Completion Rate** - Percentage of meals completed
- **Weekly Progress** - Visual progress bar

### Meal Plan Structure

Each day includes:
- **Breakfast** - Morning meal (~300-400 kcal)
- **Lunch** - Midday meal (~400-500 kcal)
- **Dinner** - Evening meal (~500-600 kcal)
- **Snack** - Healthy snack (~100-200 kcal)

### Tips

- **Plan Ahead** - Review your weekly plan on Sunday
- **Meal Prep** - Prepare meals in advance for busy days
- **Flexibility** - It's okay to swap meals between days
- **Consistency** - Aim for 80%+ completion rate

## Workout Plan

Track your weekly workout routine.

### Features

#### AI-Generated Plans

1. Click **"Generate AI Plan"** button
2. AI analyzes your health stats
3. Receives personalized workout recommendations
4. Plan automatically updates with AI suggestions

**Note:** Requires Gemini API key to be configured.

#### Weekly Schedule

Default 7-day plan includes:
- **Monday** - Chest & Triceps
- **Tuesday** - Back & Biceps
- **Wednesday** - Cardio & Core
- **Thursday** - Legs & Shoulders
- **Friday** - Full Body
- **Saturday** - Core & Flexibility
- **Sunday** - Rest Day

#### Tracking Exercises

1. **Expand a day** to see exercises
2. **Click exercise card** to mark complete
3. **View details:**
   - Exercise name
   - Sets and reps
   - Form tips and descriptions

### Points System

- **Complete exercise:** +5 points
- **Skip exercise:** -5 points
- **Complete full day:** Bonus points!

### Progress Tracking

Monitor your progress:
- **Total Points** - Workout achievement score
- **Weekly Completion** - Exercises completed this week
- **Progress Bar** - Visual representation of weekly progress

### Tips

- **Warm Up** - Always warm up before exercising
- **Form First** - Focus on proper form over heavy weights
- **Rest Days** - Don't skip rest days - recovery is important!
- **Progressive Overload** - Gradually increase difficulty
- **Listen to Your Body** - Rest if you feel pain or excessive fatigue

## AI Chat

Get personalized health advice from your AI coach.

### How to Use

1. **Type your question** in the input box
2. **Press Enter** or click **"Send"**
3. **Wait for response** (loading indicator shows AI is thinking)
4. **Continue conversation** - AI remembers context

### What You Can Ask

#### Health Questions
- "How can I improve my sleep quality?"
- "What are good sources of protein?"
- "How much water should I drink daily?"

#### Fitness Advice
- "What exercises are best for beginners?"
- "How can I build muscle?"
- "What's a good cardio routine?"

#### Diet Guidance
- "What should I eat for breakfast?"
- "How can I reduce sugar intake?"
- "What are healthy snack options?"

#### Personalized Recommendations
- "Based on my stats, what should I focus on?"
- "Am I getting enough exercise?"
- "How can I improve my overall health?"

### AI Context

The AI knows your current stats:
- Daily step count
- Calories burned
- Water intake
- Sleep duration

This allows for personalized, context-aware advice!

### Tips

- **Be Specific** - More specific questions get better answers
- **Follow Up** - Ask follow-up questions for clarification
- **Take Notes** - Save important advice for later reference
- **Verify** - Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice

### Limitations

- AI provides general health information, not medical diagnosis
- Responses are based on general knowledge, not your medical history
- Always consult a doctor for serious health concerns

## Tips & Best Practices

### Data Persistence

All your data is saved locally in your browser:
- ‚úÖ Works offline (except AI features)
- ‚úÖ Private - no data sent to servers
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Clearing browser data will erase your progress
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Data is device-specific (not synced across devices)

### Maximizing Points

**Daily Habits:**
- Complete all 6 habits = 80 points/day
- Consistency bonus: Complete 7 days in a row

**Diet Plan:**
- Complete all meals = 40 points/day
- Weekly goal: 280 points

**Workout Plan:**
- Complete all exercises = varies by day
- Weekly goal: Complete 6 out of 7 days

### Building Consistency

1. **Set Reminders** - Use phone reminders for habits
2. **Start Small** - Begin with 2-3 habits, add more gradually
3. **Track Streaks** - Monitor your completion streaks
4. **Celebrate Wins** - Acknowledge your progress!

### Mobile Usage

**Best Practices:**
- Keep app open for step tracking
- Grant all necessary permissions
- Use landscape mode for better workout plan viewing
- Save to home screen for quick access

### Troubleshooting

**Steps not counting?**
- Ensure motion permissions are granted
- Check if device has accelerometer
- Try stopping and restarting tracking

**AI not responding?**
- Verify API key is configured in `.env`
- Check internet connection
- Look for error messages in chat

**Data lost?**
- Check if browser data was cleared
- Verify you're using the same browser/device
- Consider exporting data regularly (feature coming soon)

### Privacy & Security

- All data stored locally in browser
- API key stored in environment variables
- No personal data sent to external servers (except AI queries)
- Clear localStorage to remove all data
